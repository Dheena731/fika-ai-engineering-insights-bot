Details:
- DataHarvesterAgent: Ingests GitHub/seed data, stores in SQLite
- DiffAnalyzerAgent: Reads from DB, computes DORA/churn/latency, writes analysis
- InsightNarratorAgent: Reads analysis, generates markdown via LLM, logs to DB
- Slack Bot: Reads reports/charts from DB/output, posts to Slack

LangGraph Orchestration:
[DataHarvesterAgent] --> [DiffAnalyzerAgent] --> [InsightNarratorAgent]

Storage:
- All data, analysis, and reports are persisted in [DB: SQLite]
- Charts are saved as PNGs in /output

Chat Layer:
- Slack Bot listens for slash commands, triggers pipeline, and posts results
